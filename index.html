FriendshipOnWheels

A community that connects everyone by food.

Whether you're recieving or giving, we've got you covered.

Who we are

FriendshipOnWheels is a nationwide non-profit initiative which aims to help requestors (frail, aged people and people with disability) remain in their homes by providing them with meals, friendly visits and safety checks on a regular basis. Volunteers donate their time to deliver meals, friendly interactions and safety checks, whereas Requestors/Carers are those who receive the meal.

Our goal is to connect the community through food
