Hi, I'm Adam, and I live alone with my dog and parrot. At 93, life has become a bit of a challenge, but my furry and feathery companions bring me immeasurable joy. With FriendshipOnWheels, I'm not just receiving nourishing meals; I'm also getting a daily dose of companionship and care from the volunteers who brighten my days. They've become like family, checking in on me and ensuring I'm doing well. As someone who has always valued independence, FriendshipOnWheels has allowed me to remain in the comfort of my own home while still receiving the support I need.
