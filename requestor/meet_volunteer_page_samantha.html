Hi there! I'm Samantha, a dedicated volunteer with FriendshipOnWheels. Cooking has always been my passion, and I'm thrilled to share that love by preparing delicious meals for those in need. What I enjoy most about volunteering is the opportunity to connect with the elderly recipients, sharing stories and laughter over a warm meal. Their wisdom and experiences truly enrich my life, and I'm grateful for the bonds we've formed through this wonderful program.

Chicken Soup with Croutons

My chicken soup with croutons is a heartwarming blend of tender chicken pieces, wholesome vegetables, and herbs simmered to perfection in a rich broth. I added some golden-brown croutons adds a delightful crunch and depth of flavor, creating a satisfying culinary experience that soothes the soul.

Spanish seafood paella with saffron

Nut free Gluten free

My Spanish seafood paella with saffron is a vibrant culinary masterpiece, bursting with the flavors of the Mediterranean. This traditional dish is a blend of shrimp, tender mussels, and delicate calamari, on a bed of golden saffron-infused rice. The vibrant colors and bold flavors are probably my favourite part of the dish. Perfect for those who want a bite of Spain!

Egg fried rice

Nut free Gluten free

My egg fried rice is a simple dish with aromatic flavors and a fluffy texture. Fragrant jasmine rice is stir-fried with golden scrambled eggs, crisp vegetables, and a touch of savory soy sauce. The dish is both comforting and versatile, and can be served as a standalone meal or as a delicious accompaniment to other dishes. With its irresistible aroma and irresistible taste, this egg fried rice is sure to become a favorite among diners of all ages.
